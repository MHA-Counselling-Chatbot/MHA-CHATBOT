The project aims at developing a Chatbot for legal and mental health counselling, which can understand written Hindi along with English. To reach out to India’s heart, we aim at designing this counselling bot such that it supports the mixing of languages, i.e. Hindi in English (Hinglish). The product will be an open source, pluggable, chatbot engine. This service will be provided through Facebook messenger, Android app.
